The active sequence is Rabimodulated with mw_freq swept from 3.825 to 3.925 GHz. In the provided sequence XML, full_expt is 0, so the optional 1-level reference branch is inactive. The active readouts are therefore the initial post-polarization detection as the 0-level reference, followed by a 52 ns rabi_pulse_mod_wait_time pulse and the post-pulse signal detection. The sequence uses mod_depth = 1 and length_rabi_pulse = 5.2e-08 s.

For a pODMR resonance in this sequence, I expect a reproducible frequency-dependent contrast in the post-pulse signal relative to the 0-level reference, not just an isolated raw-count fluctuation. The combined traces are noisy with only two averages. The signal-reference difference changes sign across the scan and the largest feature is a single-point upward spike in the post-pulse readout near 3.915 GHz, while neighboring points do not form a coherent resonance line shape. Candidate negative-contrast points are also not consistently supported across the two averages. I therefore do not see enough evidence for a pODMR resonance.

The two stored averages also do not give a strong independent repeatability test here and show considerable tracking-like drift; the apparent dips are not consistently reproduced as a coherent resonance line. Because the expected near-pi-pulse resonance signature is absent and the readout differences look dominated by noise/drift rather than a frequency-localized signal drop, I classify this case as resonance absent.

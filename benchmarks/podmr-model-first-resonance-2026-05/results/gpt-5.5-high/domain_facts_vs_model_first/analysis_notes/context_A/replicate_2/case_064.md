The observed data do not show that. The per-average overlay is dominated by large offset/tracking changes between stored averages, which should not be treated as a strong repeatability test. Given the near-pi pulse condition, the absence of a clear contrast-scale, reproducible dip indicates no reliable pODMR resonance is present.

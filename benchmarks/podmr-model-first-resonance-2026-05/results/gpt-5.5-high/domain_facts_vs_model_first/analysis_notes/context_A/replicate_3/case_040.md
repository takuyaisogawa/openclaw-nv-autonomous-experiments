Using the stated setup facts, mod_depth = 1 gives roughly 10 MHz Rabi frequency, so a 52 ns pulse is approximately a pi pulse. If the microwave frequency were on an NV transition, readout 2 should drop toward the m_S=+1 brightness, with a contrast scale on the order of 22% relative to readout 1. Decision: resonance_absent. There is no convincing pODMR resonance in this scan under the active sequence and pulse settings.

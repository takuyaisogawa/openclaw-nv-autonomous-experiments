If a pODMR resonance were present, the post-pulse readout should show a strong reduction relative to the 0 reference, on the order of the setup contrast scale between m_S = 0 and m_S = +1.

The signal/reference difference is small and irregular, with the largest negative combined contrast only around 5 percent and with positive excursions elsewhere. The two stored averages are not a strong independent repeatability test here because they can reflect tracking cadence; they also do not establish a consistent resonant dip. This does not look like a real pODMR resonance under the active sequence and pulse conditions.

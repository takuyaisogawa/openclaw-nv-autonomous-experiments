A pODMR resonance should appear as a frequency-localized contrast feature in the microwave readout relative to the reference, typically a consistent dip or peak exceeding the average-to-average scatter.

The combined traces are noisy and show broad drift-like variation rather than a reproducible localized resonance. The readout2-readout1 contrast changes sign multiple times and does not form a clean resonance-like dip or peak at a single frequency. I therefore classify this case as resonance absent with low confidence.

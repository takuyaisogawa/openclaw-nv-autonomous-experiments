The active pulse sequence therefore performs an adjusted polarization, takes a detection readout as the true 0-level reference, waits, applies one rabi_pulse_mod_wait_time pulse of 52 ns with mod_depth 1, then takes the signal detection readout.

Readout 1 is the pre-pulse 0-level reference. Readout 2 is the post-Rabi-pulse signal. Across the 21 mw_freq points, both traces fluctuate by a few counts with only two averages, and the post-pulse signal does not show a reproducible dip or peak relative to the reference that is localized in frequency.

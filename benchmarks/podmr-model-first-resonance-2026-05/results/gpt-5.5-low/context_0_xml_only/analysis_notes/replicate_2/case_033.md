The active sequence is Rabimodulated.xml, sweeping mw_freq from 3.825 GHz to 3.925 GHz in 5 MHz steps. The instructions first polarize and detect the true 0-level/reference readout, then wait; the 1-level reference block is inactive because full_expt is 0, despite do_adiabatic_inversion being true. The active measurement pulse is rabi_pulse_mod_wait_time with length_rabi_pulse = 52 ns and mod_depth = 1, followed by detection, so readout 1 is the reference/background and readout 2 is the microwave-pulse signal.

The reference readout remains mostly near 47-50 counts without a comparable feature, while the signal readout shows a clear localized depression around 3.875-3.880 GHz, falling from the high-40 count baseline to about 39 counts. The feature appears in both averages in the per-average overlay and is aligned with the microwave sweep rather than a common-mode reference fluctuation. This is consistent with a pODMR resonance being present.

Active sequence: Rabimodulated.xml with mw_freq swept from 3.825e9 to 3.925e9 Hz in 5 MHz steps. The sequence sets mod_depth to 1 and length_rabi_pulse to 52 ns, rounded at a 250 MHz sample rate. Because full_expt is 0, the optional 1-level reference acquisition block is inactive even though do_adiabatic_inversion is true; no adiabatic inversion is actually executed. The active readouts are therefore the initial detection after optical polarization, serving as the true 0-level/reference readout, and the later detection after the modulated Rabi pulse, serving as the microwave-driven signal readout.

The combined readouts and per-average traces show small point-to-point variation and a mild upward drift across the scan, but there is no reproducible ODMR-like dip or peak centered at a microwave frequency. The largest excursions are isolated and not consistent between readout channels or between the two averages. Given the active pODMR/Rabimodulated sequence and the expected resonance signature, this scan does not show a convincing pODMR resonance.

In the active instructions, full_expt is 0, so the optional "Acquire 1 level reference" block is skipped even though do_adiabatic_inversion is true. The executed cycle is: polarize, detection for the true 0-level reference, wait, then a rabi_pulse_mod_wait_time pulse, then detection for the signal readout. The relevant active pulse uses length_rabi_pulse = 5.2e-08 s, rounded at 250 MHz sample rate, and mod_depth = 1 from the provided sequence XML.

Thus readout 1 is the 0-level/reference readout before the microwave pulse, and readout 2 is the post-pulse readout that should carry the pODMR response. The raw combined traces over only two averages show broad point-to-point noise and drift. The post-pulse readout has local high points near the middle and upper part of the scan, while the reference readout also varies strongly and does not provide a stable baseline. The per-average overlays show large average-to-average offsets, and the apparent features are not reproducible enough to identify a clean ODMR-like dip or contrast feature tied to mw_freq.

Decision: resonance_absent. The data do not show a clear, consistent pODMR resonance under the active Rabimodulated sequence.

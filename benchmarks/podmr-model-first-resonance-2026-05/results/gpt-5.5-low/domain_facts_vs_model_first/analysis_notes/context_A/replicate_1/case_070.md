Using the given setup facts, mod_depth = 1 implies roughly a 10 MHz Rabi frequency, so a 52 ns pulse is approximately a pi pulse on resonance. If a pODMR resonance were present in this scan, the driven readout should show a clear fluorescence drop relative to the bright reference, on the order of the setup contrast scale (about 22%) near resonance. The stored averages differ substantially in baseline/trend, consistent with tracking cadence rather than a strong independent repeatability test.

Decision: resonance_absent. The scan shows drift and noise in the raw readouts, but not the expected readout-2 suppression relative to readout 1 for a resonant 52 ns, mod_depth 1 pulse.

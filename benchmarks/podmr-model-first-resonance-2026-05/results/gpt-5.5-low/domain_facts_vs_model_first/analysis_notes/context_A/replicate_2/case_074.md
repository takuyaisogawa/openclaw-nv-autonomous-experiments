The combined data show readout 2 often below readout 1, but the differences are small compared with the expected pi-pulse contrast and are not a clean localized resonance feature. The largest apparent dip near the middle of the scan is only a few counts on a roughly 50-count baseline and is not strongly repeatable across the stored averages; the per-average traces fluctuate substantially and the stored averages may reflect tracking cadence rather than independent repeatability. I therefore do not identify a reliable pODMR resonance in this scan.

With the stated setup scale of about 10 MHz Rabi frequency at mod_depth = 1, 52 ns is approximately a pi pulse on resonance, so a resonance should produce a clear transfer contrast between the reference and signal readout, on the order of the known 22% contrast scale if the pulse is effective.

## Result

The r02 pODMR completed with nonzero data and good final counts (`38.776 kcps`) but does **not** support a clear usable strong-pi ODMR resonance:

- Raw signal minimum is off-center around `3.850 GHz` (`-25 MHz` from 3.875 GHz).
- The deepest point-wise normalized point is near `+45 MHz`, but it is driven by a high reference readout denominator rather than a clean raw-signal dip.
- The reference-line-normalized residual dip is only about `6%`, while comparable fluctuations and peaks appear across the scan.
- Stored average 4 is drift-flagged by scan-order-aware drift analysis with about `31%` common-mode drop.
- A descriptive signal-only Lorentzian dip fit hits a boundary/unphysical solution, so it is not a reliable center.

Decision: reject r02 for alignment selection for now and do not use it for Ramsey/T2star.
